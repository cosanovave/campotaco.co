Our Story

Inspired by the Land,
Made for Raleigh

Campo Taco Co. was born from a deep love of vibrant Mexican flavors and a belief that great food should feel like coming home. Our kitchen draws inspiration from el campo — the countryside — where ingredients are fresh, flavors are bold, and every meal brings people together.

Located at 1001 S Saunders St in Raleigh, NC, we serve handcrafted tacos, house-made salsas, and creative cocktails in a casual, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're stopping in for a quick weekday lunch, a Friday night dinner, or a laid-back weekend meal with friends, Campo is your spot for fresh Mexican food in Raleigh.

From our slow-braised birria to our craft margaritas, every dish and drink at Campo Taco Co. is made with intention — no shortcuts, just real ingredients with real flavor, served in the heart of Raleigh, NC.
